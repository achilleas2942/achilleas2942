Hello there 👋

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science, Electrical and Space Engineering at Luleå University of Technology, Sweden. As a member of the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence research group within the Signal and System Division, I am guided by Professor George Nikolakopoulos.

I hold an Integrated Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from University of Patras, Greece, where I specialized in control and robotic systems.

My research focuses on edge computing architectures for robotic applications, aerial manipulation, and autonomy components.
